Physics Unit 5 Notes

Curtis Thompson Thursday 12th May 2016 Academics

These notes cover the compulsory topics of the AQA Physics A Unit 5 specification, and follow on from my previous Astrophysics notes which were for the optional section of unit 5. My handwritten notes on this page cover nuclear physics and thermal physics, two interesting topics. The note are now available to download.

Click here to view and download the notes. (2MB)

The topics covered in the notes are:

  • Rutherford Scattering
  • Alpha, Beta, Gamma Radiation
  • Radioactive Decay
  • Uses of Radiation
  • N-Z Graph
  • Nuclear Energy
  • Nuclear Fission
  • Nuclear Fusion
  • Ideal Gases
  • Kinetic Theory of Gases
  • Thermal Physics
