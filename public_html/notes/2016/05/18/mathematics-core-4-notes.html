Mathematics Core 4 Notes

Curtis Thompson Wednesday 18th May 2016 Academics

On this page you can download my notes for the Edexcel Mathematics Core 4 module. All of my notes are handwritten by me, and were made just for me so you may find some parts have too much detail for you, or not enough. However I chose to upload them as I'm often told how good my notes are, so thought I may as well make them available to everyone - there's no harm in that! This module covers partial fractions, vectors and binomial expansion as well as extra coordinate geometry, differentiation and integration following on from previous modules.

Click here to view and download the notes. (2MB)

The topics covered in the notes are:

  • Partial Fractions
  • Coordinate Geometry
  • Binomial Expansion
  • Differentiation
  • Vectors
  • Integration

I hope you find the notes useful. This is one of the two modules which I am resitting so you might find the information in this file is less than some of the other notes I have made. However, if you have learnt the module then the notes should be helpful and you should be alright.
