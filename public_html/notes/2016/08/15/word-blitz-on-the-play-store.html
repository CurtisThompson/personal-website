Word Blitz On The Play Store

Curtis Thompson Monday 15th August 2016 Projects and Programming

Word Blitz, my first app, is now available to download on the Google Play Store.

Click here to download Word Blitz from the Play Store.

Yay!

Are you a dictionary dummy or a word wizard? Do you think crosswords are easy? Well, maybe you need a new challenge!

Word Blitz is a game where you must try to make as many words as you can before your time runs out. Every time you make a new word you will be given a different selection of letters to make your new word from. This fast-paced game is fun to play when you have a minute or two to spare, or a good way of seeing which of your friends has the fastest vocabulary. Earn more points and get more time by making longer words to push yourselves up the leaderboards and earn over 20 different achievements.
